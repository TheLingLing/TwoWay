Overview

At our doggy daycare & hotel facility, we offer a safe and monitored environment where your pup can socialize, play or rest. It is a free roam environment with plenty of toys, treats, and couches/beds to rest on. An attendant is always with the pups and you can even check up on your pup with our live webcam streams!

PLEASE NOTE:
ALL PUPS JOINING DAYCARE MUST BE FULLY VACCINATED (COPY OF VACCINATION REQUIRED)
ALL PUPS OVER 8 MONTHS ARE REQUIRED TO BE NEUTERED/SPAYED

What we offer with our doggy daycare & hotel service:

  • early drop off requests (before 8am) through our scheduling portal at no additional cost
  • free roaming environment (NO CRATES OR KENNELS)
  • feeding (if needed) and access to clean water and natural, and organic treats
  • plenty of nylabones and safe balls to play and chew on
  • comfortable couches and beds to take a break or watch their pals play
  • outdoor potty break session in the afternoon
  • discounts for partnered buildings as well as multiple (3+) visits within the same week
